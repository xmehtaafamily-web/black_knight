Privacy Policy

Black_knight is an anonymous random chat, video chat and walkie talkie platform. We keep data collection minimal and do not require signup.

Data We Use

We may create an anonymous guest session ID, temporary device fingerprint, hashed IP safety marker, chat moderation snippets, reports, blocks, and basic usage analytics such as active users and channel counts.

Data We Do Not Store

We do not ask for passwords, OTPs, real identity documents, or account signup. We do not store camera or video streams.

Safety Logs

Reports, block actions, rate limits, spam events and moderation actions may be stored temporarily to protect users from abuse.

Cookies And Local Storage

We use cookies/local storage to keep an anonymous guest session, reconnect preferences and safety settings working.

Third Party Services

The site may use hosting, ads, affiliate links, TURN/video infrastructure and analytics providers. These services may process data under their own policies.

Contact

For privacy or safety requests, contact the site owner through the contact page.
