Safety Rules

Keep personal information private. Use Block or Report immediately if someone behaves unsafely.

Do not take screenshots or recordings of another user without consent. Report anyone who threatens to share private content.

Black_knight is for adults only. Any illegal or exploitative behavior should be reported and may lead to a ban.
