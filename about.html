About Black_knight

Black_knight is a random social platform for anonymous text chat, live video chat, walkie talkie channels, confession posts and radio-style frequency rooms.

The project is designed mobile-first with privacy-focused guest sessions, safety controls, report/block tools and dark futuristic UI.

Core Features

Random chat, random video, walkie talkie, mood matching, reconnect codes, safety moderation and admin analytics.
